About Me

Graduated with a bachelor’s degree from Southern Medical University, one of China’s top medical universities, I received systematic and specialized education in the field of biotechnology there. After graduation, I pursued a Mphil degree in bioinformatics under the supervision of Prof. Shengfeng Huang at Sun Yat-sen University, a world-class institution where I was exposed to a broader platform and my horizons were extremely expanded meanwhile.
I am engaged in data analysis related to comparative genomics, bulk transcriptomics, single-cell transcriptomics, proteomics, and microbiomics. Additionally, I am interested in developing biological data models with deep learning algorithms.
I consider myself a dedicated and self-driven professional with a strong commitment to excellence and a passion for teamwork. I excel in high-pressure environments, viewing challenges as opportunities for growth and development. I am enthusiastic about exploring innovative solutions and approaches that contribute to team success and drive impactful results.
I am currently seeking a bioinformatics-related position in my field of interest and am open to any relevant opportunities ! Check out my CV for more details about me.
